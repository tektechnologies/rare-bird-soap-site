Soap!

Our Soaps are all made by the cold process method and are scented with 100% Pure Therapeutic Grade Essential Oils and herbs. Natural powder pigment is added to some for beautiful swirls of color effect. All soaps are cured for at least 3 weeks to ensure a nice soapy lather and long lasting bar. Body and Bath Essentials!

Moisturizers- Rare Birds moisturizers range from Body Butters, Hand Salves, Lotion Bars and Hand & Body Lotion. All moisturizers are made with Organic Shea Butter, a blend of vitamin enriched oils and essential oils. All moisturizers serve a purpose depending on what your needs may be.

Sprays- Whether it's your body, bed or room; these sprays are an essential for any household. All sprays are made with Distilled Water, Witch Hazel, Vegetable Glycerine and Essential Oils. The witch hazel is a wonderful anti-inflammatory with a cooling sensation. Vegetable Glycerine is added for moisture and a carrier for oils to cling to.

Bath Salts- Our Bath salts are a blend of Dead Sea Salts, Epsom Salts and specialty salts such as Pink Himalayan Salt, Black Lava Salt and Gray Sea Salts. All nourish and Detox of body of unwanted toxins and relieve sore, tired muscles.

For the Face!

Facial Serum- Our Facial serum is pure Rose-hip Seed Oil blended with a few drops of Frankincense Essential Oil. Rose-hip Seed Oil is a dry oil that goes on like an oil when applied but soaks in nicely to leave your face feeling soft not oily. Frankincense Essential Oil is wonderful for decreasing age spots and wrinkles.

Facial Scrub- Our facial scrub is a great moisturizer plus exfoliator in one. Grape-seed oil, coconut oil and sunflower oil moisturize while crushed Red Raspberry seeds stimulate red blood cells and exfoliate away dead skin.

Facial Bar- Our facial bar is made with coconut oil, sustainable palm oil, sunflower oil and olive oil. I have added oatmeal and crushed carrot seeds for a nice soft exfoliant and carrot seed essential oil that works by stimulating red blood cells which improves appearances by adding elasticity and tone to skin. It has a cult-following as an anti-aging oil for preventing wrinkles.

WELCOME!

Rare Bird Soap Shop has many different items to choose from. Our soaps are handmade in small batches using the cold process method and made with all natural, vegan ingredients including coconut, palm, olive and sunflower oils. They are all Castille-type soaps which means, that I use mostly olive oil to create a very moisturizing lather and are all scented with essential oils that all have different healing properties. I try my best to use local and sustainable ingredients in all of my products.

Thanks for taking a look & I hope you find something you like.
